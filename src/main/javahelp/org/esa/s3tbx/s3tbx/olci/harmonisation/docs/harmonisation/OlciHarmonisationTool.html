OLCI Harmonisation Processor

Introduction

The OLCI Harmonisation Processor provides a 'harmonisation' of OLCI bands 13-15, which are the new bands in the O2A absorption line (764.4 and 767.5 nm) and the existing channel at 761.25nm. This harmonisation provides a modification of the effective transmittances to their values which would be measured at their mean wavelengths and with nominal bandwidth.
